BTC/USD Trading Opportunity Identifier with RSI

This model uses historical BTC/USD price data, a Moving Average Crossover strategy, and the Relative Strength Index (RSI) to identify potential buy and sell signals.

How Signals Are Calculated

1. Moving Average (MA) Crossover Strategy:

  • Buy Trigger (Golden Cross): A signal to **BUY** is generated when the Short MA crosses *above* the Long MA, suggesting a potential uptrend.
  • Sell Trigger (Death Cross): A signal to **SELL** is generated when the Short MA crosses *below* the Long MA, suggesting a potential downtrend.

2. Relative Strength Index (RSI) Filter:

The RSI is a momentum oscillator that measures the speed and change of price movements on a scale of 0 to 100. It helps identify overbought or oversold conditions. When the "Use RSI to Filter Signals" checkbox is enabled:

  • A **Buy** signal is only valid if the RSI is **below** the overbought level (e.g., 70), helping to avoid buying into an over-extended rally.
  • A **Sell** signal is only valid if the RSI is **above** the oversold level (e.g., 30), helping to avoid selling into an oversold bounce.
